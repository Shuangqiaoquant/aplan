# Disclaimer

APlan is an open research project for quantitative investment research, data
engineering, risk controls, paper trading, and auditability experiments.

It is not investment advice, a recommendation, a solicitation, an offer to buy
or sell securities, or a promise of return. Any example signal, candidate,
backtest result, watchlist, report, or portfolio output produced by this
project is for research and education only.

The project is currently designed to run in `research_only` mode unless a
strategy has passed the required validation, risk, and approval gates. It does
not connect to a live broker by default and should not be used for real trading
without independent validation, compliance review, and professional judgment.

Market data, announcements, and third-party datasets may be incomplete,
delayed, inaccurate, or subject to license restrictions. Users are responsible
for verifying data quality, legal permissions, regulatory requirements, and the
suitability of any research workflow for their own jurisdiction and use case.

Past performance, including backtests and paper trading, does not indicate or
guarantee future results.
