# APlan Content Operations

This directory is the semi-automated operating desk for ShuangqiaoQuant content.
The goal is to turn APlan research work into public writing, reader feedback,
and product insight without publishing investment advice.

## Workflow

```text
Research log
-> WeChat long article
-> Zhihu article or answer
-> Xiaohongshu short posts
-> Reader feedback
-> Product / research backlog
```

## Review Rule

No content should be published automatically. Every post must pass human review
using `publishing_checklist.md`.

## Directory

- `brand_rules.md`: positioning, voice, allowed claims, forbidden claims.
- `publishing_checklist.md`: pre-publish safety and quality checks.
- `calendar.md`: 4-week operating calendar.
- `posts/`: source essays and cross-platform versions.
- `wechat/`: WeChat public account drafts.
- `zhihu/`: Zhihu drafts.
- `xiaohongshu/`: short post cards and captions.
- `feedback/`: reader feedback templates and logs.
