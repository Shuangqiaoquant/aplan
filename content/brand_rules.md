# ShuangqiaoQuant Brand Rules

## Positioning

ShuangqiaoQuant is a public A-share quantitative research lab. APlan is the
first open project: an auditable research system for data, evidence, strategy
validation, risk controls, paper trading, and audit records.

## One-Line Bio

ShuangqiaoQuant 公开建设 A 股量化投研系统，记录策略如何在数据、证据、风控和审计中迭代。

## Core Message

APlan is not a stock picker. It is a tool for making investment research more
testable, auditable, and less self-deceiving.

## Voice

- Calm, evidence-based, and transparent.
- Admit uncertainty and failed validation.
- Explain methods before conclusions.
- Prefer "research observation" over "opportunity".
- Prefer "candidate" over "recommendation".

## Allowed Topics

- Data pipelines and quality checks.
- Backtest assumptions and failure cases.
- Strategy validation, risk gates, and audit chains.
- Agent-assisted research workflows.
- Open-source project updates.
- Lessons from building APlan.
- General education on quantitative research methods.

## Forbidden Claims

- "Buy this stock."
- "Guaranteed return."
- "High win rate strategy."
- "Follow me to make money."
- "Fundraising for trading."
- "Insider opportunity."
- Personalized investment advice.
- Any specific position sizing or trade instruction for readers.

## Default Disclaimer

本文仅用于研究和教育，不构成投资建议、证券推荐或收益承诺。任何候选、回测、报告和模拟盘结果都需要独立验证。

## Platform Roles

- WeChat: long-form trust and research archive.
- Zhihu: professional questions, answers, and debate.
- Xiaohongshu: lightweight discovery and visual storytelling.
- GitHub: proof of work, code, roadmap, and transparency.
