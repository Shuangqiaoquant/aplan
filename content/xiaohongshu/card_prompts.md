# Xiaohongshu Card Visual Prompts

Use these prompts to create simple image cards in Canva, Figma, Keynote, or an
image generator. Keep the cards educational and non-promissory.

## Visual Direction

- Clean research notebook style.
- White or pale neutral background.
- Accent colors: green, gold, blue.
- Use charts, checklists, audit stamps, and terminal snippets.
- Avoid luxury, money stacks, rockets, or exaggerated profit imagery.

## Card Prompt

Create a clean Chinese social media card for an A-share quantitative research
lab. Minimal layout, notebook style, subtle grid lines, small terminal window,
audit checkmark, no stock price chart promising profit. Main text: "{CARD_TEXT}".
Use green, gold, and blue accents. Professional, calm, trustworthy.
